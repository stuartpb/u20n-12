This is the mirror of the site I made for the presentations at Underwater Intervention 2012, a diving and ROV industry conference.

I maintained a structured Lua file with all the data for the conferences presentations, as well as scripts to write pages for each presentation and the overall schedule.

The pages for individual presentations can be found through the schedule listings: one is organized by time, and one is organized by track.
